Professional [football scorebug graphics](https://store.wasp3d.com/football-lower-third.html?utm_source=Github&utm_medium=Football+scoreboard) designed for live sports production, league coverage, tournament broadcasting, and digital streaming workflows.

This graphics package demonstrates a production-ready approach to presenting real-time match information, scores, game clocks, team identifiers, and competition branding within a compact scorebug format suitable for modern football broadcasts.

---

## Overview

The scorebug is one of the most persistent graphics elements in football production. Unlike lower thirds, which appear temporarily, scorebugs remain visible for the majority of the match and serve as the primary source of contextual information for viewers.

This implementation showcases a modular scorebug workflow that can be adapted for:

* Professional football leagues
* International competitions
* Youth and academy tournaments
* University sports coverage
* OTT streaming platforms
* Regional and community football broadcasts

## Design Considerations

Football scorebugs remain on screen for extended periods, making visual efficiency a critical design requirement.

Key design objectives include:

* High readability on large and small displays
* Minimal screen occupancy
* Fast information recognition
* Consistent visual hierarchy
* Broadcast-safe positioning

A successful scorebug should allow viewers to understand the state of the match within a fraction of a second.
